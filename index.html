Rake angle: 18
Splay angle: 8
The resultant angle would be 19.5 and sightline angle would be 66.6.

Rake: The angle of a part when viewed from the side of the chair.
Splay: The angle of a part when viewed from the front view of the chair.
Sightline: A point of view in which the part appears vertical.
Resultant angle: The angle of the part when viewed perpendicularly to the sightline.
*Above terminology from Peter Galbert's Chairmaker Notebook, Appendix A

T = Temperature (C)
H = Relative Humidity (%) / 100
W = 330 + 0.452T + 0.00415T2
K = 0.791 + 0.000463T - 0.000000844T2
K1 = 6.34 + 0.000775T - 0.0000935T2
K2 = K2 = 1.09 + 0.0284T - 0.0000904T2

EMC = 1800/W [ KH/(1-KH) + (K1KH + 2K1K2K2H2) / (1 + K1KH + K1K2K2H2)]
Equilibrium moisture content (EMC) is the moisture level where the wood neither gains nor loses moisture since it is at equilibrium with the relative humidity and temperature of the surrounding environment.
